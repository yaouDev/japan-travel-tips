# Additional Resources

These are actual resources August use.

[Jisho](jisho.org) - English-Japanese Dictionary
[Tabelog](tabelog.com) - Food reviews (most common in Japan - note that the rating system is extremely strict, anything above 3.2 is heavenly)
[Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps) - Maps (obviously) but also **train schedule**! Google Maps is the easiest and best updated app on trains there is (opinion)
